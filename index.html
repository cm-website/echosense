App Description

EchoSense pairs seamlessly with the echosense_v1 hardware to provide real-time obstacle detection and guidance for individuals with visual impairments or anyone in need of enhanced spatial awareness. Using advanced AI and haptic feedback, EchoSense transforms your surroundings into intuitive vibrations. Simply connect the echosense_v1 device via Bluetooth, launch the app, and start moving — EchoSense will alert you when nearby objects are detected, helping you navigate safely and confidently. Get started in just seconds — connect, scan, and let EchoSense be your sixth sense.
